The Need

a faceless society

Though we live in a society where we are connected to more people than at any other point in history, personal interaction is at an all time low. These shallow connections do little to meet the deep need we have to connect, to be heard, and to be understood.

Consider the last time you had a conversation that lasted longer than a few minutes with anyone other than a person you live with.
